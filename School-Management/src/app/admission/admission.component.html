Rules and Regulation For Admissions :

Registrations once completed does not guarantee an admission, it is subjected to various formalities like submission of admission related documents in time along with necessary fee.

Once admission is done, it is only considered completed once school receives all the necessary documents required at the time of admission along with the requisite fee. Paid Registration fees is non Refundable and non transferable.

Any detail or document if found to be wrong or falsely submitted gives school right to cancel the admission and further process is subjected to School’s management’s discretion.

A time frame is given to complete all the formalities as then only the admission process is considered completed.

Withdrawals Every admission of the child at CIS is beginning of a new relationship. Sometime for some reason or unfortunate circumstances the parents seek withdrawal of the child that time following points needs to be keeping in mind.

Before every withdrawal three calendar months notice is to be given. If notice period is not served the parents have to forfeit equivalent three months annual fee charges. Admission/Registration charges are non-refundable charges. Only security/caution money is liable to returned.

(Please note the refund is processed only after clearances of all departments). Parents are advised to refer the rules and regulations leaflet given at the time of admission for more details.
